DNA kit FAQ

Will these kits tell me everything about my health?

No. Consumer DNA tests are not a substitute for medical diagnostics. They can highlight traits, risks or interesting patterns – but you should always discuss health decisions with a doctor.

Is my DNA safe with these companies?

Each provider has its own privacy policy and data-sharing rules. Before you buy, read their latest terms on their website and decide what you’re comfortable with. Our safety guide walks through what to look for.

Can I use more than one DNA service?

Yes. Many enthusiasts start with one kit, then either upload their raw data to other platforms or order extra tests later for deeper ancestry or health insights.
