DNA kit FAQ

Will these kits tell me everything about my health?

No. Consumer DNA tests are not a substitute for medical diagnostics.

Is my DNA safe with these companies?

Each provider has its own privacy policy and data-sharing rules. Read their latest terms before buying.

Can I use more than one DNA service?

Yes. Many enthusiasts start with one kit, then upload raw data elsewhere or order extra tests later.
