Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Genectics.com, operated by Ronarn AS (Norway), handles data in full compliance with GDPR and international privacy standards. We respect your privacy and do not collect personal data unnecessarily.

1. Data we collect

Genectics.com only collects anonymised, non-personally identifiable data for analytics purposes.

  • Page views and traffic patterns
  • Browser type, country and device type
  • Anonymous event data (e.g., outbound link clicks)

No DNA or sensitive personal information is ever collected by us.

2. Cookies

We use two types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4)
  • Affiliate tracking cookies (Amazon Associates, CJ, Awin, Impact, etc.)

These cookies ensure that affiliate partners can credit us for referrals. You may disable cookies in your browser at any time.

3. Affiliate links

When you click certain outbound links (e.g., to Amazon), a tracking parameter is added to confirm that Genectics.com referred the visitor. This contains no personal information.

4. Data sharing

We share limited data only with:

  • Google Analytics – for anonymised traffic analysis
  • Affiliate networks – to confirm a purchase originated from our site

We never sell or disclose user data to third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any analytics data associated with your visits
  • Request details about how affiliate networks handle tracking
  • Use the site without accepting non-essential cookies
