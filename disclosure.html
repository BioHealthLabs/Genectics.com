Disclosure

Genectics.com is an independent comparison site focused on at-home DNA test kits for ancestry, family matching and health & wellness.

This page explains how the site is funded, how commercial links work, and how editorial independence is maintained.

1. How this site is funded

Some links on Genectics.com are commercial links. If you click one of these links and purchase a product, we may receive compensation from the merchant.

This does not increase the price you pay. Compensation helps cover hosting, maintenance and editorial work without charging visitors directly.

2. No paid rankings or sponsored reviews

All comparisons and descriptions on Genectics.com are based on publicly available documentation, provider disclosures and our editorial framework.

  • Brands cannot pay to be included.
  • Brands cannot pay to rank higher.
  • Brands cannot pay to influence scores or editorial notes.

Commercial relationships never override editorial judgment.

3. How commercial links are presented

Commercial links are typically presented as clear action buttons such as “View offer” or “Visit provider”. In some contexts, additional explanatory text may be included to clarify the relationship.

The purpose is transparency, not persuasion.

4. Editorial priorities

Our editorial focus is to help readers understand trade-offs between DNA test kits: ancestry depth, family matching, wellness features, privacy considerations and value.

  • Ratings are relative and category-based.
  • Lower-compensation products may still be listed if relevant.
  • Recommendations are driven by use case, not revenue.

5. Publisher & governance

Genectics.com is operated by ronarn AS, Norway, and is part of a broader publisher framework covering ownership, editorial standards and independence.

More information is available at: Organization.bio, Transparency.bio, and Disclosure.bio.
