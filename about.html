About Us

Genectics.com is an independent review site dedicated to providing clear, unbiased comparisons of DNA testing services. Our mission is to save consumers time and money by cutting through the marketing noise.

We are owned and operated by Ronarn AS (Org. No: 936048951), and are committed to delivering accurate, science-based information to help you make informed health and ancestry choices.

Our Goal (E-E-A-T)

Our goal is to be a dependable, easy-to-understand resource for genetic testing online. We focus on:

  • Experience: Learning from real user stories and practical use-cases.
  • Expertise: Relying on scientific sources and clear explanations.
  • Authoritativeness: Only recommending providers with solid reputations and transparent policies.
