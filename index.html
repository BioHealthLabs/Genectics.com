MyHeritage DNA: best overall starting point

If you want one kit that balances price, ethnicity detail and family matching, MyHeritage DNA is the most sensible starting point for most people.

  • Very solid ethnicity estimates, especially for European ancestry
  • Strong matching for Scandinavia, Eastern Europe, the Balkans and Jewish heritage
  • Easy-to-use interface and family tree tools
  • Frequently discounted on Amazon
Our verdict: the best “first DNA kit” for most Amazon buyers. Buy MyHeritage DNA on Amazon

AncestryDNA: best for big family trees

AncestryDNA is still the king of online family trees and cousin matching. If your main goal is to map out a huge family network and you love digging into records, this is the kit built around that use-case.

  • Access to a massive DNA database for finding relatives
  • Excellent integration with Ancestry’s historical records (subscription optional)
  • Good ethnicity estimates, but the real strength is family connections
Our verdict: the best kit if you’re serious about building a family tree. Buy AncestryDNA on Amazon

FamilyTreeDNA: for serious DNA hobbyists

FamilyTreeDNA is the kit you choose when you want to go deeper than a simple “ethnicity wheel”. It’s designed with more tools and options for people who treat genealogy as a serious hobby.

  • Strong tools for analysing matches and segments
  • Good option if you already tested elsewhere and want extra depth
  • Less “pretty”, more functional – geared towards enthusiasts
Our verdict: the right upgrade if you want to go beyond beginner level. Buy FamilyTreeDNA on Amazon

LifeDNA: health & lifestyle over ancestry

LifeDNA is different from the classic ancestry kits. Instead of focusing on where your ancestors came from, it focuses on how your genetics may influence wellness, nutrition and lifestyle factors.

  • Reports focused on wellness, nutrition and lifestyle
  • Interesting if you already know your ancestry and want something different
  • Not the right choice if your main goal is building a family tree
Our verdict: a niche but useful kit for health-focused users. Buy LifeDNA on Amazon

What about LivingDNA and 23andMe?

Two honourable mentions that are not the focus of this Amazon list:

  • LivingDNA – still one of the best options for extremely detailed British/European ancestry and a strong privacy stance. A great choice if you prioritise European detail and data protection.
  • 23andMe – still a leader in health and traits reports, but less focused on genealogy and family trees than MyHeritage DNA and AncestryDNA.
