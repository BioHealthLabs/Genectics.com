About Genectics.com

Genectics.com is an independent comparison site focused on at-home DNA test kits: ancestry, health & wellness, family matching and specialised tests. Our goal is to make it easier to choose a kit based on your actual goal, not on hype or paid rankings.

The site is published and operated by ronarn AS, a Norwegian company that runs a network of educational and comparison websites in health, genetics and personal finance.

How our comparisons work

  • We review publicly available information from each provider.
  • We compare ancestry detail, family matching tools, health / wellness features, price and ongoing subscription requirements.
  • Editorial “star ratings” are relative and for comparison only, not clinical evaluations.
  • Providers cannot pay to change scores or rankings.

Whenever we update a table or rating, it is based on new documentation, pricing changes, or product updates – not on sponsorships.

Who publishes this site

Genectics.com is part of the broader publisher framework described on Organization.bio . That page explains the ownership, editorial governance, ethics and independence rules that apply to all sites in the ronarn AS network.

In short: no external ownership, no paid placement, and clear affiliate disclosures.
