About Genectics.com

Genectics.com is an independent DNA test comparison site operated by Ronarn AS in Norway. Our goal is simple: help people understand what they are actually buying when they order an at-home DNA kit — without hype, sponsorship bias or paid rankings.

What we do

  • Compare the major DNA test brands side-by-side
  • Explain privacy, data retention and law-enforcement policies
  • Summarise ancestry, health and relative-matching features
  • Provide simple, transparent guides to help users choose safely

How we stay independent

Some pages on this site contain affiliate links (e.g. to Amazon or other merchants). These links never influence ranking or editorial content.

If you make a purchase after clicking a link, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you. This helps keep the site online and updated.
